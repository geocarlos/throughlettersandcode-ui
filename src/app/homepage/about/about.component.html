About myself

My name is Geocarlos. I have worked as an English-Portuguese translator since 2007. In 2009, with ICanLocalize, an online translation/localization service provided by OnTheGo Systems, which is a company based in Las Vegas, I started working in projects for the localization of mobile applications, especially iOS and Android apps, and websites. I have a degree in English and Portuguese language and literature, and a graduate certificate in English-Portuguese translation.

In August 2017, I completed a undergraduate program in system development. I have developed some skills in Java, JavaScript (DOM and Node, with Express) and TypeScript (with Angular and Ionic). I have learned and praticed Ionic and Cordova on my own, developing hybrid mobile apps.

Besides translation projects, I am also interested in entry level jobs as a software developer. As an English-Portuguese translator, I have a vast experience, including projects with over 100,000 words. Some of the companies I have worked with through ICanLocalize are HunterLab (website, PDF brochures), SkyFold (website), Kakao (iOS and Android app), Entec Polymers (website), Grand Hotel et de Milan, to mention just a few.
